02

For organizers & your team

Organizer OS
Everything to run the business, at /org

Live dashboard

One screen with the whole business: lead count, event pages, contributions raised, escrow held, open tasks — plus recent leads, messages, and activity.

Leads & proposals

Every enquiry becomes a tracked lead — new → quoted → booked → completed. Send titled quotes in seconds; clients accept or decline in their portal.

Milestones & escrow control

Break each event into stages with amounts. Mark funded, request release when a stage is done — the client approves the payout. Disputes freeze funds safely.

Team tasks NEW

Assign work to named team members with due dates — per event or general. Open → in progress → done; overdue flags in red; filter by person or status.

Expenses & budgets NEW

Cost lines per event — venue, catering, decor, staffing and more — moving through planned → committed → paid. Planned + committed lines ARE the budget.

Books, margins & CSV export NEW

Collected, outstanding, costs, and margin — per event and overall. Export events, payments, or expenses to CSV in one click for your accountant.

Activity trail NEW

Every status change, payment, milestone, task and team action is recorded automatically — who, what, when — on the dashboard and on every client page.

Inbox with real replies

Website messages land in your inbox with read/replied states. Reply from the dashboard — it's emailed to the sender from your own domain.

04

For clients

Client Portal
The couple's private space, at /portal

The Gather Guarantee

Payments sit safely in escrow per milestone and release only when the client approves. Unhappy? Dispute freezes the funds. Trust, made visible.

A timeline they can feel

The plan as a visual journey — every milestone with dates and progress, updated live as the organizer moves things forward.

Pay how Ghana pays

Deposits and balances by Mobile Money or card (Paystack). Every payment is confirmed, recorded, and visible — estimate, paid-to-date, and balance.

Proposals, decided in a tap

Quotes appear in the portal with a clear amount and description. Accept or decline — the organizer sees the decision instantly.

Plan now, pay over time

A financing widget turns any estimate into a gentle plan: about 30% deposit plus equal monthly instalments — clarity before commitment.

The planner, one tap away

WhatsApp the organizer straight from the portal. Email confirmations arrive at every important moment — enquiry, sign-in, payment.

05

For guests & the diaspora

Event Pages
Public celebration microsites, at /e/your-event

Gift pools, borderless

Guests contribute to the couple's pool by Mobile Money or card — with a message, optionally anonymous — and watch the goal fill up together.

Made for the diaspora

Aunties in London and cousins in Atlanta see amounts in their currency (GBP, USD, EUR…). The couple is settled in GHS. Everyone belongs.

RSVP in seconds

Yes / no / maybe with party size and a message — no app, no account. The host sees the guest list grow in real time.

The day, beautifully told

Cover story, order of events, photo gallery, venue & time — a page worthy of the celebration, in the platform's warm Ghanaian design language.

Livestream for the far-away

Drop in a stream link and the ceremony reaches everyone who couldn't board a plane — right on the event page.

Share where Ghana shares

One-tap WhatsApp sharing spreads the page through family groups. Past events become a living legacy archive. Pages can be public, unlisted, or private.

Every contribution is honest money

Gifts flow through Paystack with unique references, are verified by callback and server webhook, and reconcile idempotently — a gift can never be counted twice or lost. The organizer sees totals; the couple sees love.

“Akwaaba” isn't a slogan

Adinkra symbols, Kente accents and Twi greetings run through every page — proudly Ghanaian, never a template.

For vendors · plus the intelligence layer

07

Trust & foundations

Built to be trusted with the big day.

Passwordless by design

Single-use, 30-minute magic links; only cryptographic hashes are ever stored. Sessions are signed and locked to the browser. Nothing to phish, nothing to reuse.

Money that reconciles itself

Every pesewa is an exact integer — no rounding drift. Paystack payments verify twice (redirect + server webhook) and settle idempotently. Escrow states follow strict rules.

A memory that can't forget

The activity trail records every meaningful action with actor and timestamp, and your data leaves whenever you want it — CSV exports of events, payments and expenses.

Fast everywhere, hardened by default

Served from Cloudflare's global edge with strict security headers, content-security policy, validated inputs and parameterized queries throughout.
